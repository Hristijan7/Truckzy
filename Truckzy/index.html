Our customers describe Truckzy as a fleet management solution, accounting software for trucking companies, invocing service, and collaborative tool for charting delivery routes.
The truth is-it is all of these and much more!

Drive Hard. Organize Easy.

We at Truckzy believe that all smbs in the trucking industy should focus on the core of their business. Focus extensively on delivering, driving and delivering the goods, thus deliver value.

Having spent years in the trucking business, we realised that whn youa are runnig a small to mid sized trucking business, most of the time goes on repetive and administrative tasks, legally requered paperwork, that doesen't bring the bucks but it is madatory to do.

With a strong belief that in the 21st century data should be easily organised as a starting point, digitised, easily accessible for everyone and at a really affortable price, without any large upfront costs and investments, we started Truckzy

With the end fo goal of cutting the countless hours on addministration and paperwork, providing a digital solution for small and medium size trucking companies to work hard and organise easy, we started trukzy in 2018, launched the ready software in 2020 and got our first 5 business users, trucking companies that provided enought feedback to develope the upgrades necessary for providing a one-stop-shop digital solution, that would help all small and mid size trucking companies.

Now thaks to Truckzy, owners and managers can grow their businesses fast, guiding them with decisions made upon data that is provided in real-time, in formats and comparisons they require so the data become more valuable over time as the company grows, As for the boring paperwork, that part is done on "autopilot" so they don't have worry about missing out something.
